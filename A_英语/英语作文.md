# 英语作文

## 图表作文

### 一、关键句

1、扇形图：

- account for 90% of the total

2、柱状图/曲线图/折线图：

- decreased/increase from(数据)in(年份) to(数据) in(年份)
- In addition / by constrast ,(内容)was almost the same in(年份)and(年份)

### 二、框架

开头，图表描述：As is shown in the chart / table,  we can find that A is highest among all，[关键句]，by constrast , the lowest is B， [关键句] ，It is followed by C，[关键句]。

想象的原因：I think there are/is one/two main reasons for such great contrast, first of all, ……, secondly,,……  

结论：Based on what has been discussed above, we can conclude that .... 。I predict that the current situation will continue for a short/long time.

## 小作文

### 一、核心短句

#### 1、自我介绍部分

Welcome to our university! My name is Li Ming, a number of the Student Union【自我介绍】，I write to extend to you the warmest welcome【给你最热烈得欢迎】 on behalf of our Student Union【代表我们的学生会】.

#### 2、核心事件

On the last day of next week, we will be taking a trip to the Summer Palace【我们将去颐和园】. This is a historical site with paramount influence on our city even today. you will not only learn cool historical facts but also understand the local customs【你不仅会了解到绝妙的历史事实还将了解和欣赏到你们必然会遇到的当地习俗。】. Looking forward to meeting you all soon!

#### 3、建议信部分

Let me introduce you the tips for campus life【让我给你介绍一点小贴士，任意写点】

#### 4、感谢信

I’ve just made it back to Beijing and wish to thank you again for your warm reception in 北京【我刚刚回到Beijing和再次感谢你在北京的热情招待】。I look forward to welcoming you to my hometown. 【反手一个邀请，我很期望你能来我的家乡】。

Hope to see you soon and wish you stay well in the meantime

#### 5、邀请信

I’d like to invite you to attend <活动名>【我邀请你参加活动】。

The ceremony will be held in 地点【auditoriumn礼堂】 on 时间【June 15th, 2018, 11 a.m.-1 p.m.】The attached file contains your official letter of invitation with further information【附件中包含给您的附有更多信息的正式邀请函】。feel free to reach out with any questions and wish you stay well in the meantime.【请不要犹豫联系我们，当有疑问的时候。】

### 三、道歉信

Dear 道歉的对象,

​	I write with a bit of bad news—unfortunately, it looks like I will no longer be able【我带着一个坏消息写信——不幸的是，我似乎不能】 to  attend the 地点 next week on time【再前往A旅行，参加下周会议】

​	Long story short【长话短说】, I will have to stay in AAA for at least another two weeks to【我将不得不在北京至少再呆两周】  deal with an unexpected problem【处理一个意想不到的问题】

​	I’m really looking forward to catching up with you【我非常期待与您见面】 But fortunately, I can most definitely make that happen next month【但幸运的是，我绝对可以在下个月使这件事发生】. Would that work for you? Hope to see you soon and wish you stay well in the meantime【这个（安排）对你来说可以吗? 我为这次更改和给您带来的不便道歉。⑧希望很快能见到你!】

<p align="right">Best Regards,</p>

<p align="right">Li Ming</p>
